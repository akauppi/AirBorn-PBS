# 140 - Control Unit

The need for control in MVP 0.1:

  • speed of four wheel motors
  • central hub motor
  • battery level tracking (optional)

WiFi reception and user interface.

Need for control (MVP 0.2):

  • grabber arm motors/hydraulics
